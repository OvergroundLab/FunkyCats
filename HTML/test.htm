Hello, friends!
Welcome to Funky Cats 2.0, the revived cesspool of sequential hilarity that is more pleasant to experience than loud, intermediate buzzing. It features a bunch of different comic series all birthed from a single mind, conveniently collected in one place. Please, feel free to browse around and be astounded by the sheer stupidity of it all.

--------2017-03-07--------

Today is a historical day, for the internet has once again been blessed by the enigmatic comics of one Angelo Pizarro. Booted of the interwebs years ago, presumably because this unstoppable force of nature could not possible be contained, he has once again risen from the ashes, sharpened pencil in hand and with a reborn will to spread his filth. But let's not dwell on the past. The important thing is that you can't keep a good cartoonist down. And down he ain't. But ya'll should be down with reading silly comics and that's what this little corner of the internet will provide you, because I am somehow convinced this kind of venture is not a financial dead-end.

The plan is to re-post all the stuff from the old site I had years ago, while still providing ya'll with fresh material, making this a complete compendium of every single ink line that was ever squirted from my, err pen.

Welcome home.
